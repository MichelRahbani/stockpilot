Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is this real money? +
No. StockPilot is 100% a learning tool. You never connect a bank account or brokerage. All portfolios are virtual and all trading is simulated with fake cash.
Is the stock data real? +
Yes — prices come from Finnhub and Yahoo Finance public APIs, so they reflect real market prices. Data may be slightly delayed and should be used for learning, not trading decisions.
Is this financial advice? +
No. StockPilot is strictly educational. Nothing here constitutes financial advice. Always consult a licensed financial professional before making real investment decisions.
Do I need an account? +
No account is required to explore the app. You can load the demo portfolio and browse all features immediately. Creating an account lets you save your custom portfolio from any device.
Is it really free? +
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial period, no hidden fees. StockPilot is built for learning and is free for everyone.
What devices does it work on? +
StockPilot works on any device with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop. No app download required.
